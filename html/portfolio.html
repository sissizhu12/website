              hello!



              I'm Sissi,
              a UI/UX and product designer
              who is passionate
              about accessibility.


              my design philosophy

              comes from my undergraduate studies in psychology, computer science, and visual arts at the University of Toronto.

              I have a strong multidisciplinary understanding of human-computer interaction, especially for individuals with disabilities, low vision, or who are neurodivergent.

              additionally, my coding experience allows me to design with a better understanding of technical constraints and technical language used by coders.
